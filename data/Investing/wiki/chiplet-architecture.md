# Chiplet Architecture

Chiplet architecture is a design methodology where a large integrated circuit (IC) is decomposed into smaller, specialized functional dies, known as chiplets, which are then assembled together within a single package [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 4/5].md].

## Benefits and Functionality
The primary advantages of using chiplets include:
1.  **Improved Scalability and Yield:** By using multiple smaller dies, the overall yield of the package is improved, and the design becomes more scalable [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 4/5].md].
2.  **Cost Efficiency:** It allows for the assembly of heterogeneous dies in a cost-effective manner [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 4/5].md].
3.  **Faster Time-to-Market:** Chiplets enable selective upgrades; for example, a CPU die can remain on an older node while an AI tile jumps to a newer, smaller node, significantly reducing redesign effort [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 4/5].md].

## Industry Adoption
The trend toward chiplets is accelerating due to the flattening efficiency gains observed in traditional front-end scaling nodes [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 4/5].md].

*   **Strategic Specialization:** Chiplets allow firms to keep core Intellectual Property (IP) in-house while sourcing other components externally. This fosters multi-foundry builds and increases the value of design houses experienced in cross-foundry collaboration [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 4/5].md].
*   **Market Focus:** Advanced packaging techniques, such as 2.5D and 3D stacking, are the primary enablers for realizing the potential of chiplet designs [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 4/5].md].

## Ecosystem Implications
The growing adoption of chiplets necessitates the development of open interconnect standards and advanced packaging skills across the entire value chain [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 4/5].md].
