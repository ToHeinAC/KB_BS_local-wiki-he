# Brain-Computer Interface (BCI)

A Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) is a technology designed to translate electrical signals generated by the brain—such as thoughts, perceptions, and judgments—into external outputs, and conversely, allow external signals to influence the brain [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].

## Operational Principles
The core concept relies on:
1.  **Sensing:** Using electrodes to detect brain signals [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
2.  **Processing:** Utilizing electronic circuits to process the complex data input from the brain [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].

## Types of BCI
BCIs are categorized by their invasiveness:
*   **Invasive BCI:** These involve direct implantation into the body. Historically, deep brain stimulation (DBS) is used to treat epilepsy [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md]. Successful trials have demonstrated the restoration of movement and communication in paralyzed patients [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
*   **Non-invasive BCI:** These are less precise but more accessible. EEG (Electroencephalogram)-based technology allows users to control robotic prosthetics, track stress levels, and interact with games using brainwaves [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].

## Semiconductor Technology Drivers
The increasing complexity and sensitivity of BCI applications drive demand for specialized semiconductors:
*   **Low-Power Processing:** Due to the need for ultra-low-power operation, advanced AI accelerators, analog-to-digital converters, and amplifiers are required [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
*   **Implantable Design:** For long-term implants, reducing size, heat, and power consumption is critical, increasing the demand for ASICs tailored for BCI [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
*   **System Integration:** Low-latency processing demands SoCs that incorporate AI accelerators and Digital Signal Processors (DSPs) to interpret data into actionable intent [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
*   **Communication:** Low-power, short-distance communication chips, such as RFIC or low-power Bluetooth-low-energy (BLE), are necessary for external signal transmission [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].

## Market and Regulatory Focus
The market potential is expected to grow rapidly as BCI moves from basic research into clinical trials [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md]. Key strategic considerations include:
*   **Security:** Integrating data protection mechanisms and encryption capabilities within the SoC is crucial due to the handling of highly sensitive neural data [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
*   **Regulation:** Compliance with stringent safety validation processes, such as those from the FDA, is mandatory for invasive BCI market entry [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
*   **Interoperability:** Companies must ensure seamless integration between hardware and software by working with ecosystem partners [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
