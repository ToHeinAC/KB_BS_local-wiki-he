# Semiconductor Design

Semiconductor design is the process of defining a chip's function and circuit architecture. It is recognized as a critical determinant of product value and differentiation, particularly in high-end markets such as AI, data centers, and autonomous vehicles [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md].

## Design Evolution and Trends
The industry is undergoing several major shifts in its design philosophy:
1.  **Specialization:** There is a marked shift away from general-purpose chips toward application-specific semiconductors. This specialization is driven by the need for superior power efficiency and reliability in targeted functions [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md].
2.  **Performance Focus:** Design innovation is increasingly focused on improving performance per watt, rather than solely maximizing peak speed. Techniques like dynamic voltage-frequency scaling, chiplet partitioning, and AI-assisted thermal management are becoming standard practice [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md].
3.  **Advanced Integration:** The push for higher integration density necessitates advanced transistor architectures. The industry is progressing from FinFET to Gate-All-Around (GAA) nanosheet devices, and potentially to Complementary FETs (CFETs) or Forksheet concepts for nodes below 5 nm [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md].

## Global Design Strategies
Major global territories are adopting specialized strategies to secure technological leadership:
*   **United States:** Concentrates its efforts on Artificial Intelligence (AI) and High-Performance Computing (HPC) [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md].
*   **China:** Focuses on fostering broad in-house capabilities to achieve self-sufficiency [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md].
*   **Europe:** Aims for leadership in specialized areas, such as wide-bandgap power chips [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md].
*   **Japan:** Maintains a focus on automotive semiconductors and image sensors [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md].
*   **Korea:** Leverages its strengths in memory and foundry services while exploring emerging applications in mobile, AI, and automotive sectors [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md].

## Design Cost Drivers
Design complexity drives up development costs. IP license & royalty can consume 25–35% of a leading project’s budget. To mitigate this, companies are encouraged to build reusable in-house IP, utilize chiplets, and adopt open-source standards like RISC-V [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md].
