# Quantum Computing

Quantum computing represents a novel technological frontier that utilizes quantum mechanics principles to perform computations [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].

## Core Concepts
*   **Qubits:** Unlike classical bits, quantum bits (qubits) utilize unique superposition states, which can lead to rapid decoherence even in the presence of low noise [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
*   **Quantum Error Correction (QEC):** To effectively utilize quantum computers, it is critical to develop technologies for stabilizing qubits and implementing QEC to rectify decohered qubits [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].

## Computational Architectures
The field is characterized by the development of hybrid systems:
*   **Hybrid Quantum Computing:** This landscape combines quantum computers with supercomputers, as supercomputers are typically employed in the processes required for quantum computation [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
*   **Semiconductor Role:** The existing silicon semiconductor market is projected to remain integral to quantum computers, fostering a mutually beneficial relationship even when quantum computers reach commercialization (estimated at incorporating more than one million qubits) [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].

## Technological Paths and Semiconductor Alignment
The development path is influenced by different qubit technologies:
1.  **Superconducting Qubits:** These can leverage existing semiconductor processes, offering high scalability [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
2.  **Trapped Ions:** These offer relatively lower error rates and can operate at room temperature [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].

Chip makers are advised to align roadmaps to supply:
*   Low-latency control ASICs [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
*   Cryo-compatible interfaces [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
*   High-bandwidth links connecting quantum modules to classical HPC clusters [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].

## Applications
Quantum technologies are expected to be applied in solving complex problems in logistics and supply chain management, accelerating drug development through molecular simulations, enhancing cybersecurity through advanced encryption methods, and improving artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 5/5].md].
