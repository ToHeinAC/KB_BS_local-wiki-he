# Semiconductor Supply Chain

The semiconductor supply chain is a complex, global ecosystem encountering escalating volatility driven by geopolitical factors, localization efforts, and heightened customer expectations [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 3/5].md]. The supply chain's resilience is a key focus for global investment and strategic planning [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 3/5].md].

## Key Components
The supply chain involves several distinct, yet interconnected, functional areas:
*   **Equipment:** This category includes machines necessary for semiconductor manufacturing, such as EUV lithography [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 3/5].md].
*   **Material:** This encompasses essential substances required for chip fabrication, including wafers, chemicals, and gases [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 3/5].md].
*   **Design:** This involves defining the chip's function and circuit design [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 3/5].md].
*   **Packaging and Testing:** This stage involves interconnecting and packaging the fabricated chips into a usable form, followed by rigorous testing [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 3/5].md].
*   **Fabrication:** This core stage relies on Intellectual Property (IP) and Electronic Design Automation (EDA) tools [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 3/5].md].

## Global Resilience and Investment
Geopolitical tensions and the need for supply chain stability have spurred massive capital investments in new fabs across multiple regions [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 3/5].md].
*   **Investment Scale:** Global semiconductor fab spending from 2024 to 2030 is projected to exceed $1.5 trillion [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 3/5].md].
*   **Regional Strategies:** The United States and China are leading in spending, with China focusing on self-sufficiency using mature nodes, while the US channels capital into advanced facilities [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 3/5].md].
*   **Guiding Principle:** Through 2030, the guiding principle for investment is shifting toward **resilience** rather than pure cost reduction [pwc-semiconductor-and-beyond-2026-full-report.md [Teil 3/5].md].
