A **Notfall-Expositionssituation** is defined under the *Strahlenschutzgesetz* (StrlSchG) as an exposure situation that arises from an emergency, provided the situation does not fall under Paragraph 4 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 1/13].md].

This classification is crucial for implementing emergency response protocols and protective measures during acute radiological incidents.
