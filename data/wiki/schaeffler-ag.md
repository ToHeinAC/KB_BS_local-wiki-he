# Schaeffler AG

Schaeffler AG is a global technology company specializing in motion technology, bearings, and industrial components. The company has undergone a significant transformation, notably through its merger with Vitesco Technologies, positioning itself as an electrification powerhouse [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].

## Corporate Profile
*   **Headquarters**: Herzogenaurach, Germany [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].
*   **Ticker/ISIN**: SHA0 (Xetra) / DE000SHA0100 [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].
*   **Market Cap**: Approximately €9.5 billion [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].
*   **Employees**: Approximately 110,000 globally [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].

## Business Model and Divisions
The company's business model is structured around four core divisions following the Vitesco merger [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md]:
1.  **E-Mobility**: This division focuses on electric drives, controls, mechatronics, and e-axles, representing over 40% of automotive revenue [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].
2.  **Powertrain & Chassis**: Covers systems for both Internal Combustion Engine (ICE), hybrid, and Electric Vehicle (EV) powertrains.
3.  **Vehicle Lifetime Solutions**: Manages the aftermarket segment, providing spare parts and repair solutions [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].
4.  **Bearings & Industrial**: Handles industrial bearings, linear guides, and components for sectors such as wind, rail, and robotics [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].

The company serves a diverse customer base, including major European/Asian/German Auto OEMs (as a tier-1 supplier), and industrial clients in renewable energy and factory automation [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].

## Market Positioning and Moat
Schaeffler AG's market position is characterized by:
*   **Heritage**: A strong mechanical heritage, particularly in bearings [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].
*   **Moat**: The economic moat is currently assessed as **Narrow**, though it is expected to widen post-Vitesco integration [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md]. High switching costs for OEMs due to integrated systems contribute to this moat [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].
*   **Competitive Edge**: The company aims to compete in premium engineered segments, leveraging its Vitesco integration to close technology gaps with rivals like Bosch and ZF [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].

## Financial Health and Investment Outlook
Financially, the company reported stable revenue of €23.5B in 2025, alongside an 11% increase in EBIT to €936M [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].
*   **Strengths**: The improvement in the EBIT margin to 4.0% and the significant boost in Free Cash Flow to €266M are positive indicators [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].
*   **Weaknesses/Risks**: Significant concerns include the E-Mobility segment being projected to be loss-making (-14% to -17% margin) [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md], and high leverage, evidenced by a Debt-to-Equity ratio of 160.9% [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].

The investment thesis suggests that while the short-term outlook requires caution due to debt and segment losses, the long-term potential, driven by the EV transition and synergy realization, warrants a **BUY** rating over a 5-year horizon [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].
