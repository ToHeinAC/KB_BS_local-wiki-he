# Private Credit

In the context of investment portfolios, Private Credit refers to debt financing provided directly to private companies or assets, bypassing traditional public capital markets [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 2/2].md].

## Investment Scope
Munich Re has reported holding investments amounting to up to €2.5 billion ($2.9 billion) within the private credit sector [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 2/2].md]. This indicates a substantial allocation of capital into non-public debt instruments.
