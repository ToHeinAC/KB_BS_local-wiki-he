# Federal Office for Economic Affairs and Export Control (BAFA)

The Federal Office for Economic Affairs and Export Control (Bundesamt für Wirtschaft und Ausfuhrkontrolle, BAFA) is responsible for regulating the movement of goods across national borders concerning radioactive materials [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].

## Core Competencies
BAFA's primary responsibilities include:
* **Cross-Border Movement Permits:** Issuing permits for the cross-border movement of consumer goods, as well as their revocation and withdrawal [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
* **Monitoring:** Overseeing the monitoring of cross-border movements of radioactive substances, residues, or goods containing them [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].

## Administrative Roles
BAFA is also responsible for:
* Levying fees and expenditures for its official acts related to cross-border movements [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
