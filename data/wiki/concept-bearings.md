# Bearings

Bearings are mechanical components designed to reduce friction between moving parts, allowing machinery to operate efficiently. Historically, the bearing industry has been a foundational pillar for companies like Schaeffler AG.

## Product Scope
The concept covers a wide range of products, including:
*   Rolling bearings.
*   Linear guides.
*   Industrial bearings used in various machinery applications [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].

## Strategic Importance
For Schaeffler AG, the bearing business provides a strong mechanical heritage and a stable revenue stream through its **Bearings & Industrial** division [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md]. This segment helps cushion the impact of cyclical downturns in the automotive sector [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].

## Market Context
The market for bearings is characterized by high precision requirements and strong brand loyalty (e.g., FAG, INA, LuK) [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md]. The industry is seeing a shift, with traditional bearings being integrated into more complex, high-tech systems like e-axles and electric drives [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].
