# Smart Infrastructure

Smart Infrastructure is a business segment dedicated to modernizing and optimizing physical and energy systems within built environments and utility grids [Siemens AG — Investment Research Overview.md].

## Scope of Operations

The scope of this segment includes:
*   Grid automation solutions [Siemens AG — Investment Research Overview.md].
*   Low and Medium Voltage (LV/MV) gear technology [Siemens AG — Investment Research Overview.md].
*   Building technology and energy efficiency solutions [Siemens AG — Investment Research Overview.md].

## Financial Profile

This segment demonstrated a profit margin of approximately 15.4% [Siemens AG — Investment Research Overview.md].

## Market Role

The segment plays a critical role in supporting the transition to sustainable energy and modernizing utility infrastructure, making it a defensive component of Siemens AG's revenue base [Siemens AG — Investment Research Overview.md].
