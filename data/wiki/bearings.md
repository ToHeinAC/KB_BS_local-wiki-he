Bearings constitute the historical and foundational core of the Schaeffler AG business. The company has built its reputation over decades as a world leader in precision mechanical components [^11].

## Product Scope and Heritage
The product line is extensive, covering various applications:
*   **Industrial Bearings**: Used in general industrial machinery, including linear guides and components for wind energy and robotics [^8].
*   **Automotive Bearings**: These are critical components within the powertrain, serving both ICE and emerging EV applications.
*   **Specialized Bearings**: Schaeffler has developed specialized bearings for the electric vehicle sector, focusing on attributes such as noise reduction and high efficiency required by electric motors [^15].

## Strategic Importance
While the company is diversifying into high-tech areas like power electronics, the bearings division remains a crucial element of its "mechanical heritage" [^8]. The profitability and technological advancement within this segment are key to supporting the company's overall transformation strategy.
