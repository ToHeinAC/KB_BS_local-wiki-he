# Joule Copilot

Joule Copilot is the embedded generative Artificial Intelligence tool developed by SAP SE. It represents the forefront of SAP's technological offering and is rapidly becoming a standard feature in its cloud solutions.

## Functionality
Joule is designed to be deeply embedded across various functional areas within the SAP ecosystem, including Finance, Supply Chain, and Human Resources. Its purpose is to enhance user productivity by providing actionable, context-aware assistance directly within the workflow [SAP SE Investment Research Overview.md].

## Market Adoption
The adoption rate of Joule is a key indicator of SAP's success in the AI transformation wave. As of Q4 2025, the tool was included in two-thirds of all new cloud orders, signaling strong market acceptance and integration into the core value proposition of the Cloud ERP Suite [SAP SE Investment Research Overview.md].

## Strategic Significance
The integration of Joule signals that SAP's focus has shifted from merely facilitating system migration to driving *intelligent* operations. This positions AI adoption as the primary catalyst for future revenue growth within the enterprise software market [SAP SE Investment Research Overview.md].
