# Ambition 2030

Ambition 2030 is the comprehensive strategic plan set forth by Munich Re, announced on December 9, 2025, outlining the company's direction through the end of the decade [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]]. The strategy is designed to guide the company through a period of structural change in the insurance and reinsurance markets, emphasizing efficiency, technological integration, and superior shareholder returns.

## Core Financial Objectives (Through 2030)

The plan sets ambitious financial benchmarks designed to elevate the company's standing relative to peers:
*   **Return on Equity (ROE):** Target above 18% (significantly exceeding Swiss Re's benchmark of above 14%) [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]].
*   **Earnings Growth:** Average annual EPS growth above 8% [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]].
*   **Capital Return:** Maintaining a total payout ratio above 80% per year [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]].
*   **Solvency:** Maintaining a Solvency II ratio above 200% [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]].
*   **Return on Investment (ROI):** Target of 3.4–3.8% by 2030 (up from 3.2% in 2025) [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]].

## Operational Pillars

The strategy is built upon two major operational pillars: efficiency and growth optionality.

### 1. Efficiency Program
The plan commits to achieving €600 million in cumulative cost savings by 2030, with €200 million targeted for 2026 [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]]. This efficiency drive involves:
*   **Workforce Optimization:** Reducing approximately 1,000 positions at ERGO by 2030, primarily in standardized administrative and claims processing roles, through attrition and voluntary schemes, while committing to no operational dismissals until 2030 [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]].
*   **Reskilling:** Creating around 500 reskilling positions to leverage new technologies like AI and automation [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]].

### 2. Capital Return and Investment
The total capital return program for 2026 is set at **€5.3 billion**, comprising a dividend payout of approximately €3.1 billion and a share buyback of up to €2.25 billion [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]].

## Near-Term Milestones (2026)
The first milestone year, 2026, has specific targets:
*   Group net result: €6.3 billion (compared to €6.121 billion in 2025) [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]].
*   Group insurance revenue: €64 billion [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]].

## Implementation Focus
The execution of Ambition 2030 is heavily reliant on technological adoption, particularly AI, to automate workflows and realize the cost savings necessary to support the aggressive return targets [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2]].
