# Federal Aviation Office (LBA)

The Federal Aviation Office (Luftfahrt-Bundesamt, LBA) is the competent authority for radiation protection matters related to air travel and aviation operations [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].

## Core Competencies
The LBA is responsible for:
* **Aircraft Operations:** Reviewing the notification of aircraft operation and prohibiting such operation [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
* **Dose Calculation:** Recognizing calculation programs for determining the body dose of flight personnel [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
* **Cosmic Radiation:** Monitoring compliance with protection requirements from cosmic radiation during aircraft operations [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
* **Expertise:** Certifying expertise in radiation protection required in connection with aircraft operations, and recognizing courses for acquiring this expertise [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].

## Administrative Roles
The LBA is also responsible for:
* Levying fees and expenditures for its official acts related to aviation radiation protection [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
