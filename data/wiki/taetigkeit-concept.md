Within the framework of the *Strahlenschutzgesetz* (StrlSchG), **Tätigkeit** (Activity) refers to a broad range of actions involving radioactive materials or ionizing radiation.

## Scope of Activities
Activities include, but are not limited to:
*   Handling, acquiring, or transferring radioactive substances, or the cross-border movement thereof [StrlSchG.md [Teil 1/13].md].
*   The establishment, operation, or decommissioning of facilities [StrlSchG.md [Teil 1/13].md].
*   The operation and testing of X-ray or scatter radiation equipment [StrlSchG.md [Teil 1/13].md].
*   The addition of radioactive substances to consumer goods, pharmaceuticals, or fertilizers, and the subsequent activation of these products [StrlSchG.md [Teil 1/13].md].
*   Actions that may increase exposure or contamination from naturally occurring radioactivity, provided these actions are not already covered by other listed activities [StrlSchG.md [Teil 1/13].md].

## Definition of Activity Type
The concept of **Tätigkeitsart** (Activity Type) is defined as the totality of activities that are substantially similar when assessed under the principle of justification [StrlSchG.md [Teil 1/13].md].
