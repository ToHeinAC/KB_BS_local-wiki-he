A **Bestehende Expositionssituation** is defined within the *Strahlenschutzgesetz* (StrlSchG) as an exposure situation that already exists when a decision regarding its control must be made [StrlSchG.md [Teil 1/13].md].

This category addresses radiological risks that are present in the environment or structures prior to the initiation of specific protective actions.
