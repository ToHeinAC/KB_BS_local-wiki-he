# Physikalisch-Technische Bundesanstalt (PTB)

The Physikalisch-Technische Bundesanstalt (PTB) is a federal institute responsible for metrology and standardization within the context of radiation protection [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].

## Core Competencies
The PTB's technical responsibilities include:
* **Type Approval:** Providing type approval for scatter sources and type approval under § 45 Paragraph 1 Number 2 to 6 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
* **Measurement Quality:** Implementing quality assurance measures for external exposure measurement sites [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
* **Standards:** Providing radioactivity standards for comparison measurements [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].

## Administrative Roles
The PTB is also responsible for:
* Levying fees and expenditures for its official acts related to radiation protection [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
