A Bauartzulassung (Type Approval) is a regulatory authorization required for certain devices and facilities that generate ionizing radiation, ensuring that they meet established safety standards before being placed on the market or used [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].

## Scope of Approval
The approval covers:
*   Devices and other apparatus incorporating radioactive substances according to § 2 Absatz 1 des Atomgesetzes.
*   Facilities for generating ionizing radiation.
*   X-ray facilities, school X-ray facilities, basic protection devices, high protection devices, full protection devices, or scatterers [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].

## Validity and Extension
The law specifies rules regarding the validity of existing approvals:
*   Approvals valid on December 31, 2018, remain valid until the expiry date stated in the approval certificate and can be extended as an approval according to § 45 Absatz 1 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   Devices whose type approval expired before December 31, 2018, but were continued to operate according to specific ordinances, may continue to operate accordingly under § 48 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].

## Required Documentation
For new applications, the required documentation is detailed in Anlage 2, including:
*   A Safety Report (Sicherheitsbericht) describing the facility and its operation, detailing associated risks, and outlining necessary equipment and measures [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   Proof that the radiation protection officer and radiation protection specialists are reliable and possess the required expertise [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
