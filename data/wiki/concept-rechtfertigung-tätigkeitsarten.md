The justification of activities (*Rechtfertigung von Tätigkeitsarten*) is a central principle in radiation protection [concept-rechtfertigung-tätigkeitsarten.md].

## Legal Requirement
Under the *Strahlenschutzgesetz* (StrlSchG), any activity involving ionizing radiation must be justified [summary-strlschg-md-teil-12-13.md].

## Implementation
The principle requires that the potential benefits of the activity must outweigh the associated risks from radiation exposure. This principle is fundamental to the overall safety framework established by the law.
