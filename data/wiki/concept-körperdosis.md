# Körperdosis

The body dose (*Körperdosis*) refers to the measured or calculated absorbed radiation dose to the entire body.

## Dose Limits and Estimation
* **Professional Limits:** The limit value for professional exposure is required to be observed starting January 1, 2019 (§ 212 Absatz 1) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
* **Population Estimation:** For estimating population exposure, § 80 must be applied starting January 1, 2019 (§ 212 Absatz 2) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
* **Emergency Planning:** In emergency planning, dose values are specified as radiological criteria for determining the appropriateness of protective measures, including dose estimates and comparison with reference values [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
