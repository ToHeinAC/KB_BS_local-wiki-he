# Railway Authority (EBA)

The Railway Authority (Eisenbahn-Bundesamt, EBA) is the competent authority for radiation protection matters concerning the transport of radioactive materials by rail [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].

## Core Competencies
The EBA's primary responsibility is:
*   **Transport Oversight:** Its competence for the supervision and permitting of the transport of other radioactive substances is governed by § 24 Paragraph 1 Sentence 2 and 3 of the Atomic Energy Act. The competence for permitting the transport of large sources is determined by § 186 Paragraph 1 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].

## Administrative Roles
The EBA is also responsible for:
*   Levying fees and expenditures for its official acts related to rail transport [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
