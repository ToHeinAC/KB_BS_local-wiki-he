# Mobility Sector

The Mobility Sector, within the context of industrial technology, refers to the division responsible for solutions related to transportation and movement [Siemens AG — Investment Research Overview.md].

## Key Offerings

The sector's offerings are diverse, covering:
*   Rail transport and train systems [Siemens AG — Investment Research Overview.md].
*   Road technology solutions [Siemens AG — Investment Research Overview.md].

## Financial Outlook

The segment is targeted to maintain a margin between 8% and 10% [Siemens AG — Investment Research Overview.md].

## Market Context

This sector is subject to cyclical exposure but remains a core component of Siemens AG's diversified industrial portfolio [Siemens AG — Investment Research Overview.md].
