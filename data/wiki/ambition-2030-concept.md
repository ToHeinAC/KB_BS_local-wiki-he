# Ambition 2030

Ambition 2030 is Munich Re's comprehensive strategic plan, announced on December 9, 2025, outlining the company's direction through the end of the decade [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2].md]. The strategy is designed to ensure sustained profitability and efficiency improvements in a volatile risk environment.

## Core Financial Targets (Through 2030)
The plan sets aggressive financial benchmarks designed to outperform industry peers:
*   **ROE:** Target above 18% (compared to Swiss Re's benchmark of above 14%) [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2].md].
*   **EPS Growth:** Average growth above 8% per year [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2].md].
*   **Payout Ratio:** Maintaining a total payout ratio above 80% annually [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2].md].
*   **Solvency:** Maintaining a Solvency II ratio above 200% [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2].md].

## Operational Pillars
The strategy is built on three main pillars:
1.  **Efficiency Program:** Targeting €600 million in cumulative cost savings by 2030. This includes reducing approximately 1,000 positions at ERGO by 2030, primarily through attrition and voluntary schemes, while simultaneously creating ~500 reskilling positions to leverage AI and automation [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2].md].
2.  **Capital Return:** A total commitment of €5.3 billion to shareholders in 2026, comprising a dividend of ~€3.1 billion and a share buyback of up to €2.25 billion [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2].md].
3.  **Growth Focus:** Strategic expansion into high-growth areas, notably Cyber Risk and Asia-Pacific markets [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2].md].
