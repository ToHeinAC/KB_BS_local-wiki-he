# Indemnity/Security (Radiation Protection)

The concept of *Deckungsvorsorge* (Indemnity/Security) mandates that adequate financial provision must be made to cover potential damages arising from the use of radioactive substances or ionizing radiation in medical research [StrlSchG.md [Teil 3/13].md].

## Requirements for Security
* **Purpose:** The security must cover the case where a person is killed or injured/impaired during the application, even if no other party is liable [StrlSchG.md [Teil 3/13].md].
* **Insurance:** Proof of existing insurance is required. The insurer must be licensed in an EU Member State or an EEA contracting state [StrlSchG.md [Teil 3/13].md].
* **Scope:** The scope of the insurance must be proportional to the associated risks. It must ensure that at least 500,000 Euro is available for the death or permanent disability of each person involved [StrlSchG.md [Teil 3/13].md].
* **Alternatives:** Alternatively, proof of insurance provided under the Medicinal Products Act or the Medical Device Act may substitute the requirement for security [StrlSchG.md [Teil 3/13].md].
