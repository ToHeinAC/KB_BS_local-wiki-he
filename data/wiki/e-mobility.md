# E-Mobility

E-Mobility refers to the segment of the automotive industry focused on electric vehicle (EV) technology and related power systems.

## Scope and Components
In the context of Schaeffler AG, E-Mobility is a core revenue stream, encompassing:
*   Electric drives, controls, and mechatronics.
*   E-axles and related components.

This segment is crucial to the company's transformation, positioning Schaeffler as an electrification powerhouse[^7].

## Market Dynamics
The E-Mobility sector is characterized by rapid technological change and high investment in power electronics and advanced bearing systems[^15]. While the transition from Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) components presents an obsolescence risk for legacy parts, it drives significant demand for specialized, high-efficiency components like those pioneered by Schaeffler in EV bearings[^15].

## Financial Considerations
The segment is noted to be highly dynamic, with projected losses of **-14% to -17%** margin in 2025, indicating that while it is strategically vital, it remains a loss-making area that requires margin improvement to validate the long-term investment thesis[^17].
