# Life and Health Insurance

Life and Health (L&H) insurance covers risks related to human mortality, morbidity, and long-term care needs. This segment is characterized by long-tail liabilities, meaning claims can extend over decades [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2].md].

## Risk Characteristics
L&H risks are fundamentally different from P&C risks because they are tied to demographic and biological trends rather than immediate physical events. Key drivers include:
*   **Mortality/Morbidity Experience:** Changes in population health trends directly impact claims payouts [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2].md].
*   **Pandemic/Epidemic Shocks:** Large-scale health crises can create sudden, correlated spikes in claims across the sector [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2].md].

## Reinsurance in L&H
Reinsurance in this sector helps primary insurers manage the long-term financial uncertainty associated with population aging and evolving medical science. The L&H segment contributes significantly to the overall reinsurance revenue stream [Munich Re (MUV2) — Full Deep-Dive Investment Report.md [Teil 1/2].md].
