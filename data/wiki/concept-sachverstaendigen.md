# Sachverständige (Expert Witnesses)

The designation of experts (*Sachverständigen*) is a regulated aspect of radiation protection oversight.

## Designation Validity
* **Duration:** Official designations of experts made before December 31, 2018, remain valid (§ 172 Absatz 1 Nummer 1, 3 oder 4) for a maximum of five years [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
