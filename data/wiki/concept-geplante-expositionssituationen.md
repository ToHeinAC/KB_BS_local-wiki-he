# Geplante Expositionssituationen

A planned exposure situation (*Geplante Expositionssituation*) refers to an exposure situation resulting from activities where an exposure is caused or can be caused [StrlSchG.md [Teil 1/13].md].

## Management in Remediation
When dealing with radioactive contaminated sites, the management of planned exposure situations requires detailed planning:
*   **Planning Requirement:** A remediation plan must include a summary of the exposure assessment for the planned activities [StrlSchG.md [Teil 9/13].md].
*   **Assessment:** The law mandates the creation of an estimation of the body dose for workers involved in remediation activities, which must be updated if the work situation changes [StrlSchG.md [Teil 9/13].md].
