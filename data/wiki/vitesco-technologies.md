# Vitesco Technologies

Vitesco Technologies is an entity that underwent a merger with Schaeffler AG.

## Merger Context
The merger with Vitesco Technologies was completed in **October 2024**[^7]. This integration was pivotal for Schaeffler AG, transforming its profile from a pure bearing specialist into a comprehensive electrification powerhouse[^7].

## Strategic Impact
The integration of Vitesco's capabilities has been critical in strengthening Schaeffler's position in the automotive sector, particularly in areas requiring advanced power electronics and integrated powertrain solutions[^7].
