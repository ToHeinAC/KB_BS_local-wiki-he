# DIN 25425-4-1:2013-06 Radionuklidlaboratoratorien – Teil 4: Regeln für den Personenschutz; Beiblatt 1: Hinweise für die Erstellung einer Strahlenschutzanweisung

This document provides guidance notes for creating a formal Radiation Protection Directive (*Strahlenschutzanweisung*) for handling radioactive sources in radionuclide laboratories [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md]. It serves as a supplement to DIN 25425-4, focusing on the administrative and procedural requirements for radiation safety management.

## General Information and Scope
*   **Full Title:** DIN 25425-4-1 Radionuklidlaboratoratorien – Teil 4: Regeln für den Personenschutz; Beiblatt 1: Hinweise für die Erstellung einer Strahlenschutzanweisung für den Umgang mit radioaktiven Stoffen in Radionuklidlaboratorien [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
*   **Effective Date:** Juni 2013 [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
*   **Purpose:** The guidance aims to provide general guidelines and suggestions for defining a Radiation Protection Directive, applicable whether the handling of open radioactive substances occurs in small labs with low activity or in buildings with multiple labs and high activity inventories [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
*   **Applicability:** The directive forms the basis for regular instruction of personnel not specialized in radiation protection [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].

## Key References
The document explicitly references the following regulations:
*   *Strahlenschutzverordnung* (StrlSchV), specifically the version from July 2001 (BGBl. I S. 1714 - 1846), last amended by Article 1 VO of October 4, 2011 (BGBl. I S. 2000) [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].

## Required Content of the Radiation Protection Directive
The *Strahlenschutzanweisung* must contain, according to DIN 25425-4, Annex D, the following 16 categories of information [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md]:

1.  **Legal Basis:** Must name the legal basis and the approval notice, stating that handling is permitted according to StrlSchV, e.g., "Genehmigungsbescheid Nr. xx/yy vom… (Datum) der Behörde … mit den Nachträgen vom …" [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
2.  **Organization:** Description of the radiation protection organization, including the Radiation Protection Responsible Person (Firma, öffentliche Einrichtung, GBR-Gesellschaft), the Radiation Protection Authorized Person (if named), and the Radiation Protection Officer (Strahlenschutzbeauftragte) [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
3.  **StrlSchV Availability:** Indication of where the StrlSchV can be viewed (e.g., posted notice or intranet availability) [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
4.  **Radiation Protection Officers:** Naming of the Radiation Protection Officers, including Name, First Name, Department, official phone, private phone, and area(s) of responsibility [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
5.  **Representation Rules:** Rules for representation during working hours, permanent representation, temporary representation, and standby duty [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
6.  **Areas of Use:** Listing of rooms where handling of radioactive substances is permitted according to the approval notice, including details on buildings, rooms, and possible activity restrictions [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
7.  **Area Marking:** Designation of the radiation protection area, mandatory marking of radiation protection areas, and the name/phone number of the responsible Radiation Protection Officer at the entrance [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
8.  **Fire Service Marking:** Marking according to fire service regulations [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
9.  **Physical Radiation Control:** Regulations for physical radiation protection control, including the implementation of RiPhyko, and the mandatory wearing of dosimeters [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
10. **Responsibilities:** Naming of responsibilities, e.g., for acquisition/issue, maintenance of devices and fume hoods, leak tests, exhaust air monitoring, bookkeeping, etc., including Name, First Name, Department, Phone, and scope of tasks [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
11. **General Rules for Activities:** General rules for activities with radioactive substances, including:
    *   Wearing protective clothing, protective gloves [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   Working under fume hoods, glove boxes, hot cells [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   Working over containment trays [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   Dose monitoring, incorporation monitoring [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   Medical monitoring of Category A personnel [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   No mouth pipetting of solutions [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   No eating, drinking, or makeup [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   Regulations for intra-company transport [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   Rules for execution and documentation of daily contamination checks [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   Regulations for transporting radioactive substances by road/rail/air [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   Maintenance and checking of measuring devices with assigned responsibilities [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   Removal of materials and objects after free measurement and consent from the Radiation Protection Officer [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   Activity ban for persons with wounds or skin diseases [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   Bookkeeping of the inventory of radioactive substances [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
    *   Responsibility rules for the acquisition and issue of radioactive substances [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
12. **Contamination Behavior:** Behavior in case of personnel contamination, according to SSK recommendations [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
13. **Cleaning Staff:** Regulations for cleaning staff, suggesting floor/window cleaning outside core working hours, and instruction of these persons [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
14. **Emergency Behavior:** Behavior in case of fire and significant events, showing reporting routes, referring to the alarm plan, plan for regular alarm drills, and regulations against disturbances and loss of radioactive substances [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
15. **Waste Disposal:** Regulations for waste disposal, including separate collection on site, labeling of waste containers, and description of the content [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
16. **Record Keeping:** Maintenance of an operational or laboratory book (*Betriebs- bzw. Laborbuch*) [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].

## Documentation of Essential Operational Processes
The directive requires signatures from the Radiation Protection Responsible Person or Radiation Protection Officer, and the Radiation Protection Authorized Person [DIN 25425-4-1_2013-06.md].
