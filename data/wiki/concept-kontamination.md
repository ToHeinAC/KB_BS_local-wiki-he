# Contamination (Kontamination)

Contamination refers to the presence of radioactive substances in the environment, part [StrlSchG.md [Teil 9/13].md].

## Management and Reference Values
The management of contamination is governed by specific legal provisions depending on the situation:
*   **Emergency Situations:** When contamination occurs in an emergency situation, the provisions for contaminated sites apply, utilizing reference values set out in § 93 Abs. 1 or § 93 Abs. 2 or 3 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 9/13].md].
*   **General Principles:** The law mandates establishing responsibility and creating remediation plans during cleanup efforts [StrlSchG.md [Teil 9/13].md].

## Public Communication
In the context of emergency planning, the public may receive recommendations regarding simple hygiene and decontamination rules following contamination [StrlSchG.md [Teil 13/13].md].
