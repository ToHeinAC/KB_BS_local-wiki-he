The *Atomrechtliche Deckungsvorsorge-Verordnung - AtDeckV* is a specific regulation detailing the provisions for financial security (Deckungsvorsorge) required under the German Atomic Energy Act (*Atomgesetz* - AtG).

## Overview
This ordinance, formally titled *Verordnung über die Deckungsvorsorge nach dem Atomgesetz*, was established on January 25, 1977 [AtDeckV.md [Teil 2/2].md]. Its purpose is to regulate the financial guarantees necessary to cover potential damages arising from nuclear activities, ensuring that compensation obligations are met even if the responsible entity cannot cover the costs.

## Key Provisions
The document outlines the structure and requirements for establishing and maintaining the necessary financial provisions related to nuclear safety and waste management [AtDeckV.md [Teil 2/2].md].
