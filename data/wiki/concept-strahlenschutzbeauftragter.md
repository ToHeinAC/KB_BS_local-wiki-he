# Radiation Protection Officer (Strahlenschutzbeauftragter)

The Radiation Protection Officer (*Strahlenschutzbeauftragter*) is a role involved in radiation protection oversight [StrlSchG.md [Teil 5/13].md].

## Role in Remediation
The role is relevant in the context of remediation, where the officer must ensure that protective measures are implemented for the protection of humans and the environment against harmful effects of ionizing radiation [StrlSchG.md [Teil 9/13].md].
