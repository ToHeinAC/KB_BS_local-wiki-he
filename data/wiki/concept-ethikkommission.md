# Ethics Committee (Radiation Protection)

In the context of radiation protection, particularly for medical research, the involvement of an Ethics Committee is mandatory for obtaining necessary authorizations [StrlSchG.md [Teil 3/13].md].

## Requirements and Composition
* **Status:** The committee must be independent, interdisciplinary, and established according to state law [StrlSchG.md [Teil 3/13].md].
* **Membership:** It must consist of medical experts and non-medical members who possess the respective required professional competence [StrlSchG.md [Teil 3/13].md].
* **Registration:** Registration with the competent authority is required, and the committee's members, procedure, and address must be listed in a published procedural ordinance [StrlSchG.md [Teil 3/13].md].

## Function
The committee provides a favorable statement regarding the research project, which is a prerequisite for the authority to grant a permit for medical research involving ionizing radiation or radioactive substances [StrlSchG.md [Teil 3/13].md].
