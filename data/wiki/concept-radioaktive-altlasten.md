# Radioaktive Altlasten

Radioactive contaminated sites (*radioaktive Altlasten*) are grounds, parts of grounds, buildings, or structures contaminated by radioactive substances.

## Remediation and Permits
* **Permit Continuation:** Permits issued before December 31, 2018, for remediation, protection, or aftercare measures on sites mentioned in Article 3 of the Unity Treaty remain valid, provided they were issued before the entry into force of the Unity Treaty or before that time but still in force [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
* **Scope:** The law governs the remediation of such sites, and the provisions for the termination of measures based on these permits are detailed [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
