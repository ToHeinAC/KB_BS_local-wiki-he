# SAP SE

SAP SE is a global enterprise software company specializing in providing cloud applications and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solutions. The company's business model is heavily reliant on the modernization of legacy enterprise systems into modern, cloud-native platforms.

## Company Overview
SAP SE operates across various sectors, providing solutions that manage core business processes for large global enterprises. The company maintains a significant global presence, operating in 180 countries with a large employee base [SAP SE Investment Research Overview.md].

## Business Model and Moat
The core strength of SAP SE is its **Strong Moat**, which is primarily built upon the high switching costs associated with its software. Replacing SAP's core ERP system is a multi-year, high-risk undertaking for any organization, resulting in an exceptionally high customer retention rate (>99%) [SAP SE Investment Research Overview.md].

## Financial Performance (As of 2025/2026 Estimates)
The company demonstrated strong financial inflection points:
*   **Total Revenue (2025):** €36.8B, showing 8% growth (or 11% constant currency) [SAP SE Investment Research Overview.md].
*   **Cloud Revenue:** Reached €21.0B, representing a 23% growth rate (or 26% constant currency), confirming its status as the largest revenue stream [SAP SE Investment Research Overview.md].
*   **Profitability:** Operating Profit reached €9.6B IFRS, marking a 111% Year-over-Year increase, alongside Free Cash Flow nearly doubling to €8.2B [SAP SE Investment Research Overview.md].
*   **Backlog:** The cloud backlog is substantial at €77B, indicating strong future revenue visibility [SAP SE Investment Research Overview.md].

## Technological Strategy: Modernization and AI
SAP's strategy is centered on guiding customers through an "unstoppable modernization cycle." This involves migrating from legacy ECC systems to the **S/4HANA Cloud** platform.
*   **S/4HANA:** This platform is positioned as the foundation for autonomous operations, moving beyond simple technical upgrades [SAP SE Investment Research Overview.md].
*   **AI Integration:** Artificial Intelligence, specifically through the **Joule** copilot, is becoming a primary growth driver. Joule is embedded across core functions (Finance, Supply Chain, HR) and is included in a significant portion of new cloud orders [SAP SE Investment Research Overview.md].

## Competitive Position
SAP remains the undisputed leader in the ERP market. While competitors like Oracle and Microsoft offer broad, integrated platforms, SAP is noted for its superior growth rate in cloud applications compared to these rivals [SAP SE Investment Research Overview.md].
