A **Planned Exposure Situation** is defined within the *Strahlenschutzgesetz* (StrlSchG) as an exposure situation that arises from activities and in which an exposure is caused or can be caused [StrlSchG.md [Teil 1/13].md].

This concept is used to regulate exposures that are anticipated due to specific, planned activities, distinguishing them from exposures arising from emergencies or pre-existing conditions.
