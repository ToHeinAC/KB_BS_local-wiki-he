# Sonstige bestehende Expositionssituationen

This concept refers to existing exposure situations that are not classified as traditional radioactive contaminated sites (*radioaktive Altlasten*) or those arising from immediate emergency contamination.

## Legal Basis
The management of these situations is covered under specific provisions of the Radiation Protection Act (StrlSchG) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 9/13].md].

## Management Principles
The law mandates that when such situations are identified, the competent authority must take necessary measures to determine the cause, scope, and extent of the situation, and to assess associated occupational and population exposures [StrlSchG.md [Teil 9/13].md].

## Responsibility
Responsibility for the situation lies with the manufacturer, supplier, transporter, or owner of the radiation source causing the situation, or the holder of actual control over that source [StrlSchG.md [Teil 9/13].md].
