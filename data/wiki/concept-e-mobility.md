# E-Mobility

E-Mobility refers to the segment of the automotive industry focused on electric vehicle (EV) technology, encompassing the necessary components and systems for electric propulsion.

## Scope and Components
In the context of Schaeffler AG, the E-Mobility division is a core revenue stream, representing over 40% of the automotive business [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md]. This segment involves:
*   Electric drives and controls.
*   Mechatronics systems.
*   E-axles.

## Industry Significance
The transition from Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) vehicles to EVs is fundamentally reshaping the automotive supply chain [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md]. Companies specializing in this area, such as Schaeffler, are integrating expertise from traditional mechanical components (like bearings) into high-tech power electronics and drive systems [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md].

## Financial Considerations
While crucial for future growth, the E-Mobility segment is noted to be challenging in the short term, with projected losses ranging from **-14% to -17%** margin in 2025 [Schaeffler AG – Investment Overview.md]. The success of this division is critical to the company's long-term valuation.
