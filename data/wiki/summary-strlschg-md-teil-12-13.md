# Strahlenschutzgesetz - StrlSchG (Teil 12/13)

This section of the Radiation Protection Act (StrlSchG) details numerous transitional provisions, ensuring the continuity of permits, authorizations, and established practices that were granted under previous legal frameworks, particularly those expiring before the current law's full implementation. It also contains detailed annexes outlining technical requirements and emergency planning.

## Transitional Provisions (Sections § 202 - § 217)

The source details the continuation of various authorizations and activities:

* **Permits/Authorizations:**
    * **§ 202 (Genehmigungsbedürftige Beschäftigung in fremden Anlagen oder Einrichtungen):** Permits issued before December 31, 2018, remain valid until the specified date, and at the latest until December 31, 2023 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    * **§ 203 (Anzeigebedürftige Beschäftigung...):** Notifications made before December 31, 2018, remain valid according to § 26 Absatz 1 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    * **§ 204 (Genehmigungsbedürftige Beförderung radioaktiver Stoffe):** Permits issued before December 31, 2018, remain valid if the required expertise (§ 29 Absatz 1 Nummer 2) is proven by December 31, 2021 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    * **§ 205 (Medizinische Forschung):** Permits and notifications for medical research conducted before December 31, 2018, remain valid under the respective sections (§ 31 or § 32) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    * **§ 206 (Zusatz radioaktiver Stoffe und Aktivierung):** Permits issued before December 31, 2018, remain valid (§ 40 Absatz 1). A return concept (§ 41 Absatz 1 Nummer 3), previously not required, is only necessary for consumer goods manufactured from December 31, 2019, until that date [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    * **§ 207 (Grenzüberschreitende Verbringung von Konsumgütern):** Permits issued before December 31, 2018, remain valid (§ 42).
    * **§ 208 (Bauartzulassung):** Building type approvals (*Bauartzulassungen*) for devices and installations valid before December 31, 2018, remain valid until the expiry date stated on the certificate, and can be extended (§ 46 Absatz 5 Satz 2) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    * **§ 210 (Anzeigebedürftige Tätigkeiten):** Notifications made before December 31, 2018, remain valid (§ 56 Absatz 1) if the required documents were submitted by December 31, 2020.
    * **§ 211 (Bestellung von Strahlenschutzbeauftragten):** Appointments made before December 31, 2018, remain valid (§ 70 Absatz 1) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    * **§ 212 (Grenzwerte für beruflich exponierte Personen):** The limit value (§ 78 Absatz 2 Nummer 1) must be observed starting January 1, 2019. § 80 must be used for estimating population exposure starting January 1, 2019 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    * **§ 213 (Zulassung der Früherkennung):** Approvals for voluntary X-ray series examinations for transmissible diseases remain valid (§ 84 Absatz 4) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    * **§ 214 (Anmeldung von Arbeitsplätzen in Innenräumen):** Notifications made before December 31, 2018, remain valid (§ 129 Absatz 1), provided measures to reduce Radon-222 exposure are taken by December 31, 2020. Measurements conducted before December 31, 2018, fulfill the measurement obligation (§ 127 Absatz 1) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    * **§ 215 (Radioaktive Altlasten):** Permits issued before December 31, 2018, for remediation, protection, or aftercare measures remain valid, provided they were issued before the entry into force of the Unity Treaty or before that time but still in force [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    * **§ 216 (Bestimmung von Messstellen):** Official designations of measurement sites made before December 31, 2018, remain valid (§ 169 Absatz 1) if the prerequisites of § 169 Absatz 2 are proven by December 31, 2020 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    * **§ 217 (Bestimmung von Sachverständigen):** Official designations of experts made before December 31, 2018, remain valid (§ 172 Absatz 1 Nummer 1, 3 oder 4) for a maximum of five years [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].

## Annexes Summary

* **Anlage 1 (zu § 5 Absatz 32):** Defines residues (*Rückstände*). These include sludge/deposits from oil/gas extraction, gravel/sands/resins from groundwater treatment, unprocessed phosphate gypsum, and dust/slag from phosphate processing. Residues are also defined as excavated soil/rubble if they contain residues defined in Satz 1 and are removed according to § 64 or § 141. Materials are *not* residues if their specific activity for U-238 and Th-232 decay chains is below 0.2 Bq/g, and they are not used as construction products or introduced as raw materials in technological processes [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
* **Anlage 2 (zu § 16, § 25 Absatz 2, § 40 Absatz 4, § 46 Absatz 1):** Lists required documents for permit applications, categorized by the specific section of the Act (§ 12 Abs. 1 Nr. 1/2, § 12 Abs. 1 Nr. 3/4, § 12 Abs. 1 Nr. 5, § 25, § 40 Abs. 1, § 42 Abs. 1). Key requirements include safety reports, technical plans, proof of competence for Radiation Protection Officers and Radiation Protection Managers, and proof of compensation security.
* **Anlage 3 (zu § 55 Absatz 1):** Lists occupational fields (§ 55 Absatz 1) including processes involving thorium or uranium, such as welding with thorium-coated electrodes, handling thorium-containing optical components, and processing pyrochlore ores [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
* **Anlage 4 (zu § 97 Absatz 5):** Lists preliminary federal emergency plans (e.g., recommendations from the Radiation Protection Commission regarding accident scenarios, dose limits, and public information guidelines) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
* **Anlage 5 (zu § 98):** Outlines essential elements of the general federal emergency plan, covering legal bases, responsibilities, reference values, reference scenarios, and optimized protection strategies (including dose criteria and criteria for triggering alarms) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
* **Anlage 6 (zu § 99):** Outlines essential elements of the special federal emergency plans [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
