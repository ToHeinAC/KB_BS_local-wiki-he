This summary details the twelfth and thirteenth parts of the *Gesetz zum Schutz vor der schädlichen Wirkung ionisierender Strahlung* (StrlSchG), focusing heavily on transitional provisions for regulations enacted before December 31, 2018, and detailing specific requirements for various applications and accident planning.

## Transitional Provisions (Historical Continuity)

The document contains numerous sections detailing the continuation of previous regulations:

*   **§ 202 (Genehmigungsbedürftige Beschäftigung in fremden Anlagen oder Einrichtungen) (§ 25):** A permit issued before December 31, 2018, remains valid until the date specified in the permit, and at the latest until December 31, 2023 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 203 (Anzeigebedürftige Beschäftigung im Zusammenhang mit dem Betrieb fremder Röntgeneinrichtungen und Störstrahler) (§ 26):** A notification made before December 31, 2018, remains valid [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 204 (Genehmigungsbedürftige Beförderung radioaktiver Stoffe) (§ 27):** A permit issued before December 31, 2018, remains valid if the required expertise (§ 29 Abs. 1 Nr. 2) is proven by December 31, 2021 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 205 (Medizinische Forschung (§§ 31, 32)):**
    *   Applications for radioactive substances or ionizing radiation for medical research that were permitted before December 31, 2018, remain valid as permits (§ 31) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    *   Applications that were notified before December 31, 2018, remain valid as notifications (§ 32) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    *   Applications for medical research initiated before December 31, 2018, are concluded according to the regulations applicable before that date [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    *   Registrations of Ethics Committees (§ 92 Strahlenschutzverordnung) remain valid as registrations under § 36 Abs. 1 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 206 (Genehmigungsbedürftiger Zusatz radioaktiver Stoffe und genehmigungsbedürftige Aktivierung) (§ 40):** A permit issued before December 31, 2018, remains valid as a permit under § 40 Abs. 1 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 207 (Genehmigungsbedürftige grenzüberschreitende Verbringung von Konsumgütern) (§ 42):** A permit issued before December 31, 2018, remains valid as a permit under § 42 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 208 (Bauartzulassung) (§ 45):**
    *   Type approvals for equipment, facilities, or X-ray sources valid before December 31, 2018, remain valid until the expiry date stated in the approval certificate and can be extended under § 46 Abs. 5 Satz 2 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    *   Equipment whose type approval expired before December 31, 2018, but which was continued to be operated according to the previous regulations, may continue to be operated accordingly [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 209 (Anzeigebedürftiger Betrieb von Luftfahrzeugen) (§ 50):** Activities commenced before December 31, 2018, requiring notification under § 50, may continue if the notification was made by December 31, 2020 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 210 (Anzeigebedürftige Tätigkeiten) (§ 56):** Notifications made before December 31, 2018, remain valid if the required documents were submitted by December 31, 2020 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 211 (Bestellung von Strahlenschutzbeauftragten) (§ 70):** An appointment made before December 31, 2018, remains valid as an appointment under § 70 Abs. 1 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 212 (Grenzwerte für beruflich exponierte Personen; Ermittlung der Exposition der Bevölkerung) (§§ 78, 80):** The limit value under § 78 Abs. 2 Nr. 1 must be observed starting January 1, 2019, and § 80 must be applied for determining the population exposure starting January 1, 2019 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 213 (Zulassung der Früherkennung) (§ 84):** A permit for voluntary X-ray series examinations for determining transmissible diseases in regions or for population groups with above-average incidence of disease, issued under the previous regulations, remains valid as a permit under § 84 Abs. 4 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 214 (Anmeldung von Arbeitsplätzen in Innenräumen) (§ 129):**
    *   A notification made before December 31, 2018, for a workplace assignable to a field mentioned in Annex XI Part A of the Strahlenschutzverordnung remains valid, provided that measures to reduce Radon-222 exposure, if required by § 128 Abs. 1, are taken by December 31, 2020 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
    *   A measurement of Radon-222 activity concentration conducted before December 31, 2018, as part of an assessment under § 95 Abs. 1 in conjunction with Annex XI Part A, fulfills the measurement obligation under § 127 Abs. 1 [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 215 (Radioaktive Altlasten):** Permits issued before December 31, 2018, for remediation, protection, or aftercare measures on sites mentioned in Article 3 of the Unification Treaty (BGBl. 1990 II S. 885, 889), or for decommissioning and remediation of uranium ore mining sites, remain valid if issued before or after the Unification Treaty came into force, but are still ongoing [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 216 (Bestimmung von Messstellen) (§ 169):** Official designations of measurement sites made before December 31, 2018, remain valid as designations under § 169 Abs. 1, provided that it is proven by December 31, 2020, that the requirements of § 169 Abs. 2 are met [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **§ 217 (Bestimmung von Sachverständigen) (§ 172):** Official designations of experts made before December 31, 2018, remain valid as designations under § 172 Abs. 1 Nr. 1, 3, or 4 for a maximum of five years [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].

## Annexes Summary

*   **Anlage 1 (zu § 5 Absatz 32):** Defines "Rückstände" (residues), including materials from oil/gas extraction, groundwater treatment, phosphogypsum, etc. Residues are also defined as materials resulting from the intentional accumulation of materials listed in Sentence 1, or excavated/removed soil/rubble containing residues [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **Anlage 2 (zu § 16, § 25 Absatz 2, § 40 Absatz 4, § 46 Absatz 1):** Details the required documentation for permit applications, divided into Parts A through G, covering safety reports, expert personnel proof, and specific application details (e.g., medical application, consumer goods).
*   **Anlage 3 (zu § 55 Absatz 1):** Lists specific activity fields (§ 55 Abs. 1), including welding with thiored electrodes, handling of thorium-containing optical components, and waste management from mining/metallurgy.
*   **Anlage 4 (zu § 97 Absatz 5):** Lists documents concerning preliminary federal emergency plans, including recommendations from the Radiation Protection Commission (e.g., guidelines for public information, dose assessment criteria) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **Anlage 5 (zu § 98):** Outlines essential elements of the general federal emergency plan, covering legal basis, coordination, reference values, reference scenarios, and optimized protection strategies (e.g., dose assessment criteria, public information guidelines) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 12/13].md].
*   **Anlage 6 (zu § 99):** Outlines essential elements of special federal emergency plans (content not detailed in the provided text).
