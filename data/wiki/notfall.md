A Notfall (Emergency) in the context of radiation protection refers to an event that can result in significant adverse effects from ionizing radiation [summary-strlschg-01-md].

## Classification and Response
The law addresses emergency situations, which are categorized as regional or local, depending on whether their adverse effects are expected to be limited primarily to the area of occurrence [summary-strlschg-01-md].

## Procedural Oversight
The management of emergency situations is governed by specific protocols, distinct from routine planned activities. The assessment of potential risks during an emergency must still adhere to the overarching principles of dose limitation and optimization, even in crisis scenarios [summary-strlschg-02-md].
