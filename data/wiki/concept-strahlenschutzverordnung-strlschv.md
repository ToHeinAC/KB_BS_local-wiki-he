The *Strahlenschutzverordnung* (StrlSchV) is the detailed implementing regulation for the *Strahlenschutzgesetz* (StrlSchG) [summary-strlschg-md-teil-12-13.md].

## Key Areas Regulated
The StrlSchV details the operational and procedural aspects of radiation protection, covering topics such as:
*   **Dose Limits and Monitoring:** It specifies rules for dose measurements and monitoring [summary-strlschv-md-teil-3-15.md].
*   **Contamination Control:** Procedures for contamination control are detailed, including the requirement for immediate measures if measured values exceed permissible contamination limits according to § 57 (2) StrlSchV [summary-din-25425-4-2019-12-md-teil-1-2.md].
*   **Waste Management:** It governs the handling and documentation of radioactive residues and waste [summary-din-25425-4-2019-12-md-teil-1-2.md].
*   **Facility Operation:** It provides detailed guidelines for the operation of radiation protection areas [summary-strlschv-md-teil-12-15.md].
