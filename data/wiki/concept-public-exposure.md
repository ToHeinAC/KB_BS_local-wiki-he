Public exposure refers to the incidental or planned exposure of individuals in the general population [concept-public-exposure.md].

## Regulatory Context
The *Strahlenschutzgesetz* (StrlSchG) distinguishes between professional exposure and public exposure, ensuring that the protection measures are tailored to the source and nature of the exposure [summary-strlschg-md-teil-12-13.md].
