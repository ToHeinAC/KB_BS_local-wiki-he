# Federal Radiation Protection Agency (BfS)

The Federal Radiation Protection Agency (Bundesamt für Strahlenschutz, BfS) is a key federal authority responsible for implementing and enforcing the German Radiation Protection Act (StrlSchG) in several critical areas [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].

## Core Competencies
The BfS holds primary responsibility for:
*   **Medical Research:** Issuing, reviewing, and revoking permits for the application of radioactive substances or ionizing radiation to humans for medical research purposes [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md]. It also handles the prohibition of such applications [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
*   **Operational Oversight:** Reviewing the notification of the application of radioactive substances or ionizing radiation to humans for medical research, and prohibiting such applications [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
*   **Facility Approval:** Granting type approvals for devices containing radioactive substances and facilities for generating ionizing radiation [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
*   **Dose Measurement:** Implementing quality assurance measures for determining the body dose of flight personnel [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
*   **Cosmic Radiation:** Monitoring compliance with protection requirements from cosmic radiation during spacecraft operations [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
*   **Registers:** Establishing and maintaining registers for Ethics Committees evaluating research projects, occupational exposures, and high-radioactivity sources [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
*   **Justification:** Reviewing the justification of activity types, including those involving consumer goods or type-approved devices [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
*   **Radon:** Recognizing sites for measuring Radon-222 activity concentration and conducting quality assurance measures for internal and Radon exposure measurements [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].

## Administrative Roles
The BfS is also responsible for:
*   Providing guidance on the electronic submission of applications and notifications, ensuring compliance with data protection regulations (e.g., GDPR) [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
*   Levying fees and expenditures for various official acts related to radiation protection [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].

## Penal Enforcement
The BfS is listed as a competent administrative authority for enforcing penalties related to radiation protection violations, including failure to report, operate, or measure correctly [StrlSchG.md [Teil 11/13].md].
