Eine Röntgeneinrichtung ist eine Vorrichtung oder ein Gerät, in der oder dem Röntgenstrahlung mit [StrlSchG.md].

## Technical Requirements for Use
The operation of X-ray facilities is subject to strict technical requirements to ensure patient safety [StrlSchV.md]:
*   **Display Function:** The facility must have a function that displays the parameters for determining the exposure received by the examined or treated person, or, if not possible by state-of-the-art technology, a way to determine the received exposure otherwise [StrlSchV.md].
*   **Recording Function:** It must have a function that electronically records the parameters required for determining the exposure of the examined or treated person and makes this electronically usable for quality assurance [StrlSchV.md].
*   **Intervention Use:** If used for fluoroscopy during interventions, it must have a function that continuously displays the parameters for determining the exposure of the examined person [StrlSchV.md].

## Quality Assurance and Checks
The *Strahlenschutzverantwortliche* must ensure several mandatory quality checks:
*   **Acceptance Test (§ 115):** Before commissioning, an acceptance test must be conducted by the manufacturer or supplier to ensure the required quality for the application [StrlSchV.md].
*   **Constancy Check (§ 116):** The facility must be regularly checked after commissioning to ensure that the required quality is still achieved (constancy check) [StrlSchV.md].
*   **Record Keeping:** Records of the acceptance tests and constancy checks must be maintained [StrlSchV.md].
