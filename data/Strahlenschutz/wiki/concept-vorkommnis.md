# Vorkommnis (Incident)

In the context of radiation safety, a *Vorkommnis* (Incident) is a defined event that triggers specific regulatory responses.

## Definition
A *Vorkommnis* is defined as an event occurring within a planned exposure situation that has resulted in, could result in, or might result in an unintended exposure to ionizing radiation or radioactive substances [StrlSchV.md]. If the event is deemed irrelevant for radiation protection, no incident exists [StrlSchV.md].

## Regulatory Context
The identification and reporting of incidents are crucial components of radiation protection management:
*   **Reporting:** The law establishes procedures for the reporting of significant incidents (*Meldung eines bedeutsamen Vorkommnisses*) [StrlSchV.md].
*   **Assessment:** The StrlSchV provides criteria for determining the significance of an incident, particularly in planned exposure situations (Anlage 15) [StrlSchV.md].

## Distinction from Accidents
While related to accidents (*Störfall*), a *Vorkommnis* specifically refers to an event within a *planned* exposure situation that leads to unintended exposure, whereas a *Störfall* relates to a failure of the facility or activity itself [StrlSchV.md].
